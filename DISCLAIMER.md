## Purpose

Yotsuba Intel is a research and educational tool for open-source intelligence (OSINT) analysis of publicly available data from 4chan's public JSON API. It is intended for:

- Security researchers studying anonymous community behavior
- Journalists investigating public interest stories
- Law enforcement or investigators with lawful authority
- Academic research under appropriate ethical oversight
- Personal threat intelligence and self-research
