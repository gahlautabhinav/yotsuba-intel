# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Contributors and maintainers of this project pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experience
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community
- Showing empathy toward other contributors

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, trolling, or insulting comments
- Public or private harassment of any kind
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Using this project or its tools to target, harass, or harm real people
- Any other conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Ethical Use Requirement

This project is an OSINT research tool. Contributors must not use, demonstrate, or advocate for use of this software in ways that:

- Target private individuals without lawful basis
- Facilitate stalking, doxxing, or coordinated harassment
- Violate applicable privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
- Support mass data collection for commercial profiling without consent

Pull requests that add features designed to enable these behaviors will be rejected.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer at the contact listed in the repository. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct may be permanently removed from the project team.
